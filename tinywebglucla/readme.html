Piano Hero Instructions

By: Robert Posada, John Stucky, Christopher Thai

Piano Hero is an interactive piano playing simulator. It has 2 modes, a free play mode and game mode, which is similar to Guitar Hero. Our advanced topics include picking, "pseudo-collision" detection and bump mapping.

Controls

All of the piano keys are mapped to keys a-k and w-u. The details are below:

Key Piano Note
a C
w C#
s D
e D#
d E
f F
t F#
g G
y G#
h A
u A#
j B
k C

The keys '1', '2', '3' switch between the 3 octaves you can play. You can also click on a key to play it. The '4' key will turn bump mapping on/off.

Free play

Do whatever you want! Mess around, learn how to play piano, whatever.

The game part

You can select either a premade song ("Ode to Joy" or "Mary Had a Little Lamb") or practice mode. Either way, squares that line up with each white key will start to fall down. Play the corresponding key at the right time and the squares will turn green. Otherwise, the square will turn red if you didn't correctly play the note. Once each song is done, you can select a mode to start playing again!
